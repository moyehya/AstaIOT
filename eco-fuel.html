WHAT IT IS ?

Fuel level sensor is designed for precision fuel level measurement in all kinds of vehicles tanks, also in tanks of fixed installations. It is used as a standard fuel level sensor or as an additional sensor to be used with tank monitoring systems or vehicle tracking systems. It facilitise the user to monitor the fuel economy and other related imformations of fuel tanks.

The system equipped with GPS tracker which used to live track the vehicle used in Logistics and Level Monitoring Sensor for Fuel to get output in desired form to the system, which enable the administrator to take necessary actions.

SYSTEM OVERVIEW

This is fuel monitoring system mainely customized to use in fuel tankers, Petrol bunks, etc... to monitor the fuel level, detect fuel theft and to determine fuel consumption along with live movement and location and other aspects of vehicle, when fuel tank is ported from place to place.

BENEFITS

ACCURATE MEASUREMENTS
Light weight and easy to install Fuel level sensors measure and shows accurate readings of fuel.
VEHICLE TRACKING
Fuel Level Monitoring with Telematics enable tracking of logistic vehicles which ports fuels.
FUEL CONSUMPTION
Reports from sensors used to monitor the level of fuel and rate of consumption from the container.
PANIC BUTTON
Panic Button to be used by the vehicle driver, who transport fuel, in case of emergency like theft.
ANTI-THEFT ALERTS
An Alarmic Alert and voice call through triggered panic button, makes Asset owner to get alert of the situation.
REMOTE IMMOBILIZATION
This feature from the user control, provide the engine cut-off to avoid fuel theft or hijacking of asset.
