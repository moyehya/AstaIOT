WHAT IT IS ?

Temperature Monitoring System is an advanced monitoring system of temperature in living environment or any container in which controlling the temperature is desired. Generally Temperature is known as rise or fall in heat, and it is a measurement, which can be measured by simple thermometer for our better understanding. Here, in our system, we use same concept accompanied by advanced communication technology.

In our system thermosensor (thermometer) sense the heat in the environment or container and sends output signal in our desired format that can be transferred to server communication system to work with, anywhere and at any time.

SYSTEM OVERVIEW

Monitoring of Temperature is just simple system, which measure the temperature reading at our desired place and through which we get digital output data. The system provide alerts and reports according to the control set we've given to the system. Other control accessories (optional) can be triggered to take necessary action accordingly.
